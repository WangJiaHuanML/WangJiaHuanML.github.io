Biography

I am currently a Ph.D. student at the College of Computer, National University of Defense Technology, under the supervision of Assoc.Prof. Tao Sun, Prof. Kaijun Ren, and Prof. Dongsheng Li. Previously, I received my B.S. degree from the Department of Mathematics at Liaoning Technical University in July 2021, and my M.S. degree from the Department of Mathematics at Huazhong Agricultural University in July 2024.

My research interest includes machine learning theory and deep learning.
